Our Tours

Discover the world with Kesh Cruises and Travel Agency. From the ancient wonders of Uzbekistan to breathtaking destinations in over 20 countries, our tours are designed to inspire and delight. Whether you're seeking cultural heritage, natural beauty, or new adventures, we offer carefully curated experiences tailored to your travel dreams.

Dubai – United Arab Emirates

Spend 7 days and 6 nights exploring the vibrant city of Dubai! Visit world-famous attractions like the Burj Khalifa, Palm Jumeirah, and traditional markets, enjoy a thrilling desert safari, and experience the luxury and culture of the UAE. Price: Starting from $700 per person – includes accommodation, city tours, and more.

Paris – France

Experience the magic of Paris in 8 days and 7 nights. Visit iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame, stroll along the Seine, and enjoy world-class cuisine and culture in the City of Light. Price: Starting from $1000 per person – includes accommodation, guided city tours, and select meals.

Pyramids of Egypt – Cairo, Giza

Uncover the mysteries of ancient Egypt with a journey to the majestic Pyramids of Giza, including the Great Pyramid, the Sphinx, and the Valley Temple. Explore thousands of years of history and marvel at one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. Duration: 6 days and 5 nights. Price: Starting from $850 per person – includes accommodation, guided tours, and museum visits.

Molokini Crater – Maui, Hawaii

Spend 7 days and 6 nights in tropical paradise exploring Molokini, a stunning crescent-shaped volcanic crater off the coast of Maui. Enjoy snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and breathtaking island views. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Price: Starting from $700 per person – includes accommodation, Molokini boat tour, snorkeling gear, and guided excursions around Maui.
