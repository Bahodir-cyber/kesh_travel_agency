Travel throughout Uzbekistan

Discover the Heart of Central Asia on a 7-Day Journey! Explore the timeless beauty of Uzbekistan as you travel through its most iconic cities. Begin in vibrant Tashkent, then uncover the ancient wonders of Samarkand, the architectural gems of Bukhara, and the open-air museum city of Khiva. This unforgettable week-long adventure blends rich history, breathtaking architecture, and the warm hospitality of the Uzbek people.

About the tour

This 7-day tour includes a domestic flight (Tashkent–Samarkand, Bukhara–Khiva), comfortable intercity transfers by bus, hotel accommodation with breakfast, traditional meals in each city, guided excursions in Uzbek, Russian, and English, travel insurance, and all entrance fees to historical sites and attractions.

1–2 Days: Tashkent\n3–4 Days: Samarkand\n5–6 Days: Bukhara\n7th Day: Khiva

The price for the 7-day tour is $700 per person, including all flights, transfers, accommodations, meals, tours, and insurance.
